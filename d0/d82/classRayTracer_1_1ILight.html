Detailed Description

Pure-virtual interface for scene light sources.

All light implementations must inherit from ILight and provide concrete behavior for querying type, intensity, direction, and position relative to a surface interaction point.

Compute the incoming light direction at a surface point.

For directional lights, this is constant. For point lights, this is the normalized vector from the hit point toward the light.
