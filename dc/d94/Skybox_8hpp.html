Detailed Description

Declares a minimal PPM-based skybox loader and sampler.

Author
Cyrian Pittaluga
Version
1.0
Date
2025-05

The module provides two ways to obtain background radiance for rays that miss all geometry:

  1. Textured skybox – a single lat-long PPM image loaded at start-up;
  2. Procedural gradient – a simple horizon-to-zenith blend.

The active mode depends on whether initializeSkybox succeeds.
